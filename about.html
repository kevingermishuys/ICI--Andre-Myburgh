Who We Are

Impartial Verification, Every Cargo, Every Time

Independent Cargo Inspectors (ICI) provides impartial, third-party inspection and survey services at the Port of Walvis Bay — one of the most strategically important ports in southern Africa, serving Namibia and the wider SADC hinterland.

We work on behalf of importers, exporters, trading companies, shipping lines, freight forwarders, insurance underwriters and P&I Clubs who need reliable, independent verification of cargo quantity and condition — free from any conflict of interest with shippers, carriers or receivers.

Every survey is carried out on-site by our inspectors and backed by detailed, defensible reporting that stands up to international scrutiny, giving our clients the confidence to trade with certainty.

Our Values

What Sets Us Apart

Four principles guide every inspection we carry out.

Independence

We act solely on behalf of accuracy and fact — never on behalf of a shipper, carrier or receiver. No conflicts, no bias.

Accuracy

Precise measurement and documentation — from draft readings to moisture testing — using proven, industry-standard methods.

Integrity

Reports that reflect exactly what we observe, presented with the evidence to support every finding.

Responsiveness

Vessels don't wait — neither do we. Fast turnaround on inspections and reporting, around the clock.
