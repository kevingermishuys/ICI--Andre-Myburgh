Marine & Cargo Inspection Services

A comprehensive suite of independent inspection and survey services covering every stage of cargo handling at the Port of Walvis Bay and across the SADC region.

01

Draft Survey

Precise ship draft readings before and after loading/offloading to accurately determine cargo weight — the gold standard for bulk cargo quantity verification.

02

Grain Inspection

Full inspection services for wheat, maize, sorghum and other grains including sampling, grading, moisture testing, infestation checks and phytosanitary requirements.

03

Container Inspection

Pre-loading and on-receipt container surveys including seal verification, damage assessment, and stuffing/stripping supervision for FCL and LCL cargo.

04

Hatch Survey

Hatch condition reports on vessel arrival and departure, documenting pre-existing damage and cargo residue to protect all parties in the trade.

05

Sampling & Analysis

Representative sampling of bulk cargo for laboratory analysis, with coordination through accredited labs for quality certificates accepted internationally.

06

Weighbridge & Tally

Independent weighbridge supervision, truck tally services, and outturn reports for all bulk and break-bulk commodities discharged or loaded at port.

07

Liquid Bulk & Petroleum

Shore tank ullage surveys, ship tank measurements, temperature and density readings, and quantity calculations for liquid bulk cargoes including petroleum products.

08

Break-Bulk & Project Cargo

Condition surveys, lashing and securing inspections, and outturn reports for steel, machinery, vehicles, timber and all general break-bulk cargoes.

09

Reefer & Perishables

Temperature monitoring, pre-trip inspections, continuous data logger reading and condition surveys for refrigerated containers and perishable cargo.

10

Damage & Loss Assessment

Independent cargo damage surveys for insurers, cargo owners and P&I Clubs. Detailed reports with photographic evidence and causation analysis.

11

Transit & Border Inspection

Inspection of transit cargo destined for the SADC hinterland, including sealing, condition documentation and cross-border compliance checks.

12

Certificates & Reports

Issuance of quantity and condition certificates, letters of protest, hatch open/close records, time sheets and all documentation required in international trade.
